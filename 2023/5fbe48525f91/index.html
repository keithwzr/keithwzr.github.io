Writing Task2 Lecture 2

1. Question Types

“软条件”

The use of social media will cause privacy invasion. (too strong)

a) Hedging — use ‘soft’ language 软化中心句语气

the use of social media may increase the risk of privacy invasion.

e.g.

b) 中译英

使用社交媒体对社会的主要负面影响是隐私侵犯的风险。

the main negative impact that the use of social media has on society is the risk of privacy invasion.

六分操作

the use of social media may increase the risk of privacy invasion.

将中心句拆成逻辑

… use social media —> ? —> …invasion of their privacy

? : 用户的个人信息被运营这些网站的公司收集

to elaborate, 我认为造成这个问题的主要原因是 当人们使用社交网站时，他们的个人信息(例如真实姓名、身份证ID、手机号码等)会被运营商收集，随之带来这些信息被泄露的可能。 — 正向

if 人们不使用这些平台，他们的个人信息就不会被平台的运营商收集，也就不会带来信息被泄露的风险了 —反向

for example, 我曾经使用过一些社交网站，不久之后我就接到了大量的骚扰电话，对我的生活造成了很大的影响；我的很多朋友、同学也有过类似的经历 —举例

therefore, 我非常坚定地认为人们使用社交媒体可能导致他们的个人信息被泄露 —重述

The use of social media may increase the risk of privacy invasion. to elaborate , i think the main cause of this problem is when people use social networking sites, their personal information(such as their real name, ID number, phone number, etc.) will be collected by the companies running the websites, which probably cause the leakage of this information. if people don’t use these platforms, their personal information won’t be collected and the leakage of this information won’t happen then. For example, I’ve used some social networking sites, like Facebook and Twitter, and soon after received a lot of sales calls and fraud calls , which have had a huge negative impact on my daily life; many of my friends also have such experience. therefore , I firmly believe that people using social media would cause the leakage of their personal information.

Exam tip: 中译英，每个步骤(正、反、举例、重述)只能写一句话

七分操作

思路变化如下

… use social media —> How? —> 用户的个人信息被运营商收集 —> How? —> …invasion of their privacy

当人们使用这些网站 —> 首先要注册 forced to register，这意味着他们需要填写一些个人信息，例如真实姓名，身份证ID，手机号码等等。(additionally) 这些网站也会收集用户的浏览记录以及搜索记录 browsing history and search history. 甚至，平台可能非法监听、监控用户的电子设备 be monitored illegally 以获取更多隐私信息 —> 他们的个人信息被运营这些网站的公司收集 —> 这些公司可能会泄露用户的个人信息，因为他们可以从中获利。(Additionally, even through) 这些公司和他们的员工没有泄露用户的信息，这些信息也可能被黑客盗取和泄露 —> …invasion of their privacy 这就是我为何会认为使用社交网站可能导致个人信息的泄露。

Elaboration 具象化 = logos/deductive reasoning 演绎：

a) 从不同的维度（至少两个）

b) 一步一步，剥丝抽茧地解释一个动作如何最终造成这一结果和现象。

线性思维 Linear reasoning

Linear thinking/thinker:

Start point(SP) —> stage 1 —> 2 —> 3 —> … —> End point(EP)

vs

Non-linear thinking:

Multiple SPs —> stage 1.1/1.2/1.3… —> … —> … —> EP

‘*They are able to traverse the many directions to the problem that may have and find the central root of the problem, rather than trying to solve the surface symptom…avoid over/hasty-generalization…*’
